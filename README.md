## Social Media Content Process

For social media content planning, I follow this process:

```text
1. Understand the brand, audience, and content goal
2. Identify content themes and post categories
3. Plan weekly or monthly content ideas
4. Write captions with clear messaging
5. Prepare visual content direction or design brief
6. Organise posts into a content calendar
7. Review content for consistency, clarity, and engagement
